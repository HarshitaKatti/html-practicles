recipe to make tea

  1. Boil water
  2. Add tea leaves or a tea bag
  3. Steep for a few minutes
  4. Remove tea leaves or tea bag
  5. Pour into a cup
  6. Add milk and sugar if desired
  7. Stir and enjoy your tea!
